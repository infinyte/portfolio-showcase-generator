## Why Technical Work Is Hard to Showcase

### The Translation Gap

Engineers speak in code, architectures, and systems. Recruiters speak in impact, results, and value. Bridging this gap is exhausting:

- **Technical details** need to become **business outcomes**
- **Code complexity** needs to become **problem-solving stories**
- **System architecture** needs to become **career achievements**

### The Time Investment

Creating quality portfolio materials takes:
- **8-12 hours** per project to write compelling content
- **2-4 hours** to create architecture diagrams from scratch
- **3-5 hours** crafting interview stories that land
- **Days** building a portfolio website

Most engineers skip it. Or rush it. Or do it poorly.

Different audiences need different content:

| Audience | What They Care About | What You Show Them |
|----------|---------------------|-------------------|
| Recruiters | Business impact, clear results | Technical implementation details |
| Engineers | Architecture, trade-offs | Generic feature lists |
| Executives | Strategic value, ROI | Code complexity |

**Mismatch = missed opportunities.**
